FEATURES

LegendCraft features a full stable economy, physics, ability to fly, shoot a gun, and compete in gamemodes. As well as a full array of commands. LegendCraft allows for a full customization of ranks, worlds, and the server itself.

HISTORY

LegendCraft was founded in summer of 2012 (july 24th), where it started out as a nameless 800Craft custom edition software forked from 800craft v.206. Eventually, LeChosenOne, the sole developer at the time gave it the name, LegendCraft. After the first release, it was quickly found out by a few servers, most notably by Rising Embers.
